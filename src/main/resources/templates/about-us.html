About Us

Who are we?

Dining Room 1234 NBCC Moncton Campus

Dining Room 1234 is the practical learning experience for students in the Hospitality Department at Moncton Campus to show their outstanding potential while working in our amazing cafeteria.


What Do We Offer?

Every week we feature a unique banquet style 3 course meal inspired in different nationalities and cultures.

  • We operate during breakfast, lunch, and dinner times, each one with different menus
  • Check out our menus to see which banquet you would like to enjoy

  • We recommend booking a reservation in advance, as we usually sell out
  • * Reservations can be made for a maximum of 8 people
  • Vegetarian? Vegan? Any allergies or special requests? No problem! We are here to make everybody enjoy this experience! Just let us know while filling out your reservation form.
